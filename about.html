About UbwubatsiHub

Our Story

UbwubatsiHub is a web-based collaborative platform designed to bridge the knowledge gap between local and international construction professionals in Rwanda. The platform facilitates knowledge exchange about sustainable construction practices, preserves traditional building techniques, and promotes modern sustainable solutions.

Today, we're proud to serve a growing community of professionals who contribute to and benefit from our shared knowledge base. Our platform will become a trusted resource for building practices, guidelines, and technical documentation.

Our Mission

To empower architects and civil engineers with accessible, high-quality building resources and foster a collaborative community dedicated to excellence in construction practices.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for building knowledge sharing, setting standards for professional collaboration and resource accessibility in the architecture and civil engineering community.
